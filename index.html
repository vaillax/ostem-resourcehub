Out in Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics

A home for LGBTQ+ students in STEM at Michigan.

oSTEM builds community for LGBTQ+ students and allies across the University of Michigan: social events, mentorship, professional development, and a hub for the campus resources that matter most. Everyone is welcome here.
